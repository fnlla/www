# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people
- Provide actionable, evidence-based feedback
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks

## Scope

This policy applies in project spaces and community channels related to Fnlla.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to `conduct@fnlla.co.uk`.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that do
not align with this code of conduct.
